Deep Space Sync Guide

This is your personal, synchronized music player, built for Sarthak and Reechita to listen together, no matter the distance!

Search & Queue

Use the search bar to find YouTube songs, or paste links from YouTube (including Playlists) or Spotify (Track/Album/Playlist). All added songs are automatically shared in the Queue.

The Queue

Drag and drop songs in the Queue tab to re-order the shared playlist. Click a song to start playing it immediately.

Sync Status

The main player syncs both the video and the playback time. If one of you gets an ad or buffers, the other player will pause automatically (Ad/Buffer Stall) to keep you perfectly synchronized.

Chat

The chat is for real-time messages with your partner! Share your thoughts on the music.

Awaiting Partner to Resume...

Playback paused due to an ad/buffer stall by your partner. You cannot resume playback until they do.
